Ingredients:
  Sharp cheddar, shredded
  salsa
  refried beans
  cream cheese

Spread the cream cheese over the bottom of a casserole dish. Next, spread the 
refried beans over the cream cheese. After this, spread the salsa over the
beans, and then sprinkle the cheese over the salsa. Throw in the oven until the
cheese melts and go to town with some tortilla chips.
